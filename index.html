Harvard celebrates 100th anniversary

This year marks a significant milestone for Harvard University as we celebrate our 100th year anniversary. Since our founding, we have been dedicated to fostering excellence in education, research, and service. As we commemorate a century of academic excellence, we reflect on our rich history and look forward to continuing our mission of advancing knowledge and creating positive change in the world. Join us as we celebrate this momentous occasion with a series of special events, exhibits, and initiatives throughout the year!
